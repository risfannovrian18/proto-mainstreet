Our Story

More Than Just
a Meal

Main Street debuted in April 2024 as a fast-casual café envisioned to offer guests a thematic escape — a scenery of the past with a playful modern twist. We believe that every visit should leave a mark; not just on your palate, but on your memory.

Situated in the heart of Kota Wisata, Cibubur, we curate every detail — from the warmth of our lighting to the care in each dish — to create an experience that feels both elevated and effortlessly welcoming.
